Hello! We are Volunteezy.

We make it easy for Non-governmental Organizations (NGOs) to recruit volunteers suited for their needs to help make the world a better place.

Our Mission

At Volunteezy, our mission is to bridge the gap between passionate volunteers and NGOs seeking to make a positive impact. We believe that by connecting dedicated individuals with organizations in need, we can help create a better world, one volunteer at a time.

Why We Started Volunteezy

Volunteezy was founded with the belief that everyone has the power to make a difference. We noticed that many NGOs struggled to find volunteers who matched their specific needs, and many volunteers found it challenging to discover opportunities that aligned with their passions and skills. We decided to create a platform that makes it easier for both sides to connect and collaborate effectively.

Why Volunteezy is Great

Volunteezy offers a user-friendly interface that simplifies the process of finding and applying for volunteer opportunities. Our platform provides detailed profiles for both volunteers and NGOs, ensuring that the right matches are made. Volunteezy has a wide range of opportunities to choose from, ensuring you find the right volunteering opportunity for you.

We also offer various tools and resources to help NGOs manage their volunteer programs more efficiently, including volunteer management tools, event scheduling, and communication platforms.
